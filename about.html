About Us

Naaman Muses Co & Ltd is a leading provider of high-quality marine and distribution services in Oman. Established with a vision to enhance the efficiency and reliability of maritime operations, we have built a reputation for excellence and innovation.

With decades of experience, our team offers unparalleled expertise and insight into the marine and distribution industries. We operate as exclusive agents and distributors for a variety of multinational brands, ensuring that our clients receive the best products and services available.
